# EcoMark — Industrial Carbon Transparency Platform

Welcome to **EcoMark**, a stunning, full-featured compliance-grade platform engineered to deliver verifiable carbon transparency for modern manufacturing. 

This repository implements the complete **EcoMark** specification as a highly practical, self-contained, and interactive application. By integrating the visual assets generated by Stitch with a simulated database engine, this application runs entirely in the browser with **full database persistency (`localStorage`) and fully functional workflows**—no complex server configurations, Python installations, or Firebase sign-ups required.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Core Structure**: HTML5 Semantic Markup
- **Styling & Theme**: CSS3 custom variables & transitions + dynamic Tailwind CSS utility frames
- **Visual Assets**: Materials Icon fonts & high-fidelity graphics integrated from Stitch
- **Dynamic Charts**: Chart.js plotting real-time data lookups
- **Simulated Database & Auth (`js/db.js` & `js/auth.js`)**: An advanced client-side database layer that simulates a connected Flask REST API and Firestore document structure, persisting state in `localStorage` and validating sessions in `sessionStorage`

## 🧪 Interactive End-to-End Test Scenarios

Follow this step-by-step audit route to test the extreme interactivity of the EcoMark platform:

### 1. The Enterprise Flow (Carbon Calculations & Submissions)
1. Open **`index.html`** and click **Login** in the top bar.
2. Sign in as **Ravi Steel Industries** (`company1@ecomark.com` / `company123`).
3. Note your starting metrics: **Rating C**, **2,628.0 kg YTD emissions**, and **850 ECT coins**.
4. Click **Submit Utility Report** or go to the sidebar **Submit Report**.
5. Fill out the form:
   - **Quarter**: `2024-Q4`
   - **Product Name**: `Steel Rod Grade-60` *(This adds a new report to the existing product!)*
   - **Batch Quantity**: `1000` units
   - **Utility Row 1**: Select `Diesel Fuel` $\rightarrow$ Input `400` Liters
   - **Utility Row 2**: Click **Add Fuel Type** $\rightarrow$ Select `Electricity` $\rightarrow$ Input `150` kWh
6. Click **Calculate & Submit**. 
7. **Math Verification**:
   - Diesel contribution: $400 \times 2.68 = 1,072.0 \text{ kg CO2e}$
   - Electricity contribution: $150 \times 0.82 = 123.0 \text{ kg CO2e}$
   - Total CO2e: $1,072.0 + 123.0 = 1,195.0 \text{ kg}$
   - CO2e/Unit: $1,195.0 / 1000 = 1.1950 \text{ kg}$
   - Assigned Score: **Grade A** (since $1.195 \le 2.0$)
8. Review the dynamic success modal indicating **Grade A**, **1,195.0 kg total carbon footprint**, and the exact ECT coins earned!
9. Click **Go to Dashboard**. Note that your line chart trend has updated instantly to include the Q4 audit point, and your YTD emissions card has recalculated.

### 2. Earning & Spending ESG Rewards (Eco-Coins Redemption)
1. Navigate to the **Eco-Coins** tab on the sidebar.
2. Note your new transaction logs (On-Time submission reward $+50$, Rating A Achievement $+300$, and a **10%+ carbon footprint reduction bonus $+500$** because your footprint dropped from $5.256 \text{ kg/unit}$ in Q3 to $1.195 \text{ kg/unit}$ in Q4!).
3. Scroll to **Redeemable Platform Perks**.
4. Choose **Waived ESG Audit Waiver** (Cost: `800 ECT`). Click **Redeem**.
5. Verify that your wallet balance decrements immediately, a new transaction row of `-800 ECT` is recorded in the ledger, and a success popup appears!

### 3. Consumer Scanning (Public QR Codes)
1. Go to the **My Products** tab.
2. Review your registered product **Steel Rod Grade-60**, showing the new upgraded **Grade A** rating badge.
3. Click **Download QR**—it downloads the high-res custom QR code instantly to your computer!
4. Click on the QR code thumbnail to view the public consumer page (`/consumer/product.html?productId=prod_ravi_steel`).
5. **Consumer Experience Check**:
   - The page loads without auth (you are now acting as a standard store shopper!).
   - It renders **Grade A** with a green badge, calculating an equivalency statement ($1.195 \text{ kg} \times 6 \text{ km/kg} = 7.2 \text{ km}$ of passenger driving!).
   - Under **Emissions Trend**, it compares Q4 ($1.195 \text{ kg}$) to Q3 ($5.256 \text{ kg}$) and displays a verified **-77.3% footprint reduction curve**!

### 4. Regulator Center (Admin Portal)
1. Click **Log Out** to return to the gate.
2. Login as the Regulator Bureau using **`admin@ecomark.com`** / **`admin`**.
3. Review global regulator stats: **4 companies**, aggregate emissions processed, and average sector scoring curves.
4. Interact with the **Facility Emissions Registry** table: filter by sectors, or sort by high-to-low carbon footprints.
5. Click **Export Ledger (CSV)**—it exports a compiled, spreadsheet-ready report of all corporate audits in `localStorage` directly as a download!
