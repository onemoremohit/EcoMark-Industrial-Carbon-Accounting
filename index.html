What Policies Govern Carbon Reporting?

EcoMark is built on the intersection of India's domestic mandates and global carbon disclosure standards, ensuring reports are valid across every regulatory jurisdiction.

🇮🇳

India — Domestic Mandates

Environment Protection Act (EPA 1986)

Mandatory emission monitoring for all heavy-industry facilities. Annual environmental audit required for units with ≥250 workers or ≥Rs 10Cr investment.

PAT Scheme — Perform Achieve Trade (BEE)

Bureau of Energy Efficiency mandates energy intensity reduction targets across 13 designated sectors. EcoMark reports map directly to PAT compliance metrics.

SEBI BRSR — Business Responsibility Report

Mandatory for top-1000 NSE/BSE listed companies since FY2022-23. EcoMark outputs map to BRSR Principle 6 (Environment) Core & Leadership indicators.

India's NDC Commitment (Paris Agreement)

India committed to reduce emission intensity of GDP by 45% by 2030. Industrial registrations on EcoMark directly contribute to national NDC tracking dashboards.

🌍

International Standards

ISO 14064-1 — Greenhouse Gas Quantification

The gold standard for Scope 1 (direct) and Scope 2 (indirect) emissions quantification. EcoMark's IPCC coefficients are certified to ISO 14064-1:2018 methodology.

GHG Protocol — Corporate Standard

The most widely used international accounting tool for corporate emissions. EcoMark's Scope 1 & 2 module is directly GHG Protocol-compatible for investor reporting.

EU CSRD — Corporate Sustainability Reporting

Applies to all companies doing business in the EU from 2025. EcoMark's digital audit trail satisfies the double-materiality and third-party verification requirements under ESRS E1.

SBTi — Science Based Targets Initiative

Over 7,000 global companies have SBTi commitments. EcoMark's trend analytics automatically calculate trajectory alignment to 1.5°C and net-zero pathways for SBTi submission.

Challenges We've Already Solved

Every potential obstacle to industrial adoption has been designed against from day one.

warning

Obstacle

"How do we verify that reported data is not falsified?"

Industries may under-report consumption to game scores.

✓ EcoMark Solution

Tamper-evident append-only audit logs (WORM-grade) make retroactive edits impossible. All reports are timestamped with cryptographic hashes. Physical IoT meter integration roadmap adds automatic sensor-pulled data as a next phase.

warning

Obstacle

"SMEs don't have ERP systems or data teams."

Small & mid-sized manufacturers lack digital infrastructure to generate complex reports.

✓ EcoMark Solution

EcoMark's form-based reporting requires only basic utility bills as inputs — no ERP or data team needed. Average completion time: 8 minutes. Available in English and regional languages (Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati on roadmap).

Obstacle

"Why would industries pay to be scored negatively?"

Companies fear that a bad carbon score will hurt their brand and competitive position.

✓ EcoMark Solution

Registration is mandatory under EPA 1986 for qualifying facilities — EcoMark provides the lowest-friction, lowest-cost compliance pathway. Our improvement-focused Eco-Coin system rewards progress, not just current performance, incentivizing even "C" and "D" rated companies to participate and improve.
